Time Series analysis really interests me, and I’ve been involved in a lot of forecasting, demand planning and developing of forecasting models. I’ve use regression analysis and also ARIMA modeling.

A time series is a random process where observations are made at times points, t = 0,±1,±2, · · · . and is simply a sequence of random variables indexed by time. In practice, the series is usually observed at time points t = 1, 2, · · · .
